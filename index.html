About ADRONIX

ADRONIX RECORDS was founded on a simple belief: great music doesn't need permission. Born from the underground, built for the limitless — we are a record label that operates at the intersection of sonic innovation and cultural depth.


We are not just a label. We are a creative ecosystem — providing artists with distribution, visual identity, and a community of like-minded collaborators.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you submit a demo, fill out a contact form, or communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, social media links, and any audio files or documents you upload.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to review your music submissions, respond to your inquiries, and communicate with you regarding potential signings or partnerships.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is strictly used by the Adronix Records A&R and management teams.

Terms of Use

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Demo Submissions

Submitting a demo to Adronix Records does not guarantee a record deal, feedback, or any formal partnership. By submitting, you confirm that the work is entirely your own and does not infringe on any third-party copyrights.

2. Copyright & Ownership

You retain 100% ownership and copyright of your music upon submission. Adronix Records does not claim any rights to your unreleased music just by listening to your submission.

3. Website Usage

You agree to use this website for lawful purposes only and refrain from attempting to disrupt the site’s functionality or submitting malicious files through our upload portals.

Fraud Warning

Official Notice to All Artists

It has come to our attention that scammers often impersonate record label A&Rs online.

ADRONIX RECORDS WILL NEVER ASK YOU FOR MONEY.

We do not charge fees for demo listening, A&R feedback, signing contracts, or distribution setup. If someone claiming to represent Adronix Records asks you for payment via PayPal, Crypto, CashApp, or any other method, it is a scam.

All official communication will only come from our Discord Server or an official @adronixrecords.com email address.
