About ADRONIX

ADRONIX RECORDS was founded on a simple belief: great music doesn't need permission. Born from the underground, built for the limitless — we are a record label that operates at the intersection of sonic innovation and cultural depth.


We are not just a label. We are a creative ecosystem — providing artists with distribution, visual identity, and a community of like-minded collaborators.
